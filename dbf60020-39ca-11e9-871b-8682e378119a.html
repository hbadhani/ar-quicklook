How It Works

Creative Cloud Libraries lets you access and share your assets across all your Adobe Creative Cloud desktop and mobile apps.

With Creative Cloud Libraries, all your assets - colors, character styles, paragraph styles, Adobe Stock assets, and more - are synced to each of your applications.

CC Libraries ensure your designs are consistent across projects and allow you to collaborate with creative partners.
